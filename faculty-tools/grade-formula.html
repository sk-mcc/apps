Grade Formula Generator

Build the Excel letter-grade formula for a Canvas gradebook export

How to Use

  1. In your Canvas gradebook export, find the cell with the first student's final grade percentage (e.g. CJ2).
  2. Type that cell reference below.
  3. Copy the generated formula and paste it into the cell just to the right of that final grade.
  4. Drag the formula down to fill the column for every student.
Use the column letters and row number, like CJ2 or AB3.
